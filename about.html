About Us

Why we started this business?

Founded by a group of university friends in 2001. DAR started in a small garage in St. Lucy. These friends worked out of this garage selling their summer projects such as refurbished laptops and products such as headphones, mouses and other peripherals they had. Without realising these friends quickly became the number one stop in St. Lucy for all of your computer needs. After finishing university, the friends made it official and started DAR Electronics. Everyday since then, they have been working hard to serve their ever growing customer base with the most Dynamic, Affordable and Reliable products.
